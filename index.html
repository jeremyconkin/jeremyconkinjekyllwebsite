About

With a focus on empowering teams to achieve their best, I am an engineering leader and technology consultant who thrives on driving innovation and delivering world-class user experiences. My approach blends technical expertise, strategic vision, and a commitment to fostering high-performing, collaborative cultures.

Over 15 years of experience across industries—including media, gaming, and consulting—has honed my ability to lead teams in designing and implementing scalable solutions for mobile, web, XR, and cloud platforms. A user-centered approach has consistently delivered measurable outcomes, such as improving app ratings from below 2.5 stars to above 4.5/5 stars for many applications, decreasing application down-time without increasing cloud costs, and empowering organizations to release technology updates several times a day.

Technology is most effective for businesses when it accelerates the core business function. As a consultant, I strive to align technical solutions with business goals. My technical expertise includes software development in many programming languages; cloud architectures using AWS technologies like CDK and SAM; and advanced development for AR/VR experiences. I have certifications as an AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate, AWS Certified Developer – Associate, Contentful Certified Professional, and Certified ScrumMaster.
